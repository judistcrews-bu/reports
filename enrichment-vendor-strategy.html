Clay.com: Fast Data Refresh Option

What is Waterfall Enrichment?

Sequential search across multiple data providers (75+) until finding valid contact information — dramatically higher match rates than single-source vendors.

Traditional Single-Source

LeadIQ, ZoomInfo alone

  • 40-50% match rate
  • Limited to one database
  • High annual contract
  • Already eliminated

Clay Waterfall Approach

Searches 75+ providers

  • 80%+ match rate
  • Multiple data sources
  • Pay-per-use credits
  • 2-3 week setup

Use Case: Territory Planning Data Refresh

If RevOps Needs Data Urgently

  • Credits: 600,000 credits ($720 one-time or monthly)
  • Coverage: ~10,000-15,000 enriched accounts
  • Timeline: 2-3 weeks from approval to data delivery
  • Output: CSV export with updated firmographics for territory planning

Trade-off to Consider

Clay is excellent for one-time refreshes but doesn't provide ongoing automated CRM enrichment. For long-term automation, Option 1 (Clearbit/6sense) or Option 3 (in-house) are better fits.
